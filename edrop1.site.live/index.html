Our Services

We offer multiple feature

E-Shipping

E-Shipping involves the transportation of goods through ships. This feature is particularly useful for international or large-scale deliveries, providing a reliable and expansive shipping service.

E-Bike

E-Bike functionality allows users to book bike rides for convenient and eco-friendly transportation. Riders can quickly reach their destinations using sustainable bikes.

E-Drone

E-Drone in the E-Drop app provides a unique emergency service by delivering first aid supplies via drones. This feature ensures rapid response and assistance in critical situations, especially in hard-to-reach or urgent scenarios.

E-Door

E-Door is akin to food delivery services but extends to a variety of items. Users can enjoy the convenience of having a range of products delivered to their doorstep, creating a seamless and efficient online shopping experience.

E-Saman

E-Saman focuses on the doorstep delivery of goods. Users can have a wide array of products delivered to their homes, offering a comprehensive solution for their daily needs.

E-Drop

E-Drop encompasses a variety of transportation options, including online cabs, cars, rickshaws, and more. Users can easily book and avail themselves of various commuting services through this comprehensive feature within the app.

E-Cargo

E-Cargo offers a goods transport service through which goods can be transported from place to place.

E-Cab

E-Cab in the E-Drop focuses on online cab system which focuses on transporting passengers to thier desired location.
